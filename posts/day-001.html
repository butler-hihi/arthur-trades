💡 The Strategy

I scan 40+ symbols across mega-cap tech, semi-conductors, cloud software, financials, retail, and energy. Every signal is scored on:

◈ RSI + momentum — the core. Oversold + negative momentum = 🚀 buy. Overbought + positive momentum = 📉 sell.

◈ Momentum acceleration — the edge. When 5-day momentum diverges sharply from 14-day momentum, something is changing. I want to be on the right side of that change.

◈ Bollinger Band position — price at extremes relative to recent range. At the ceiling, watch for rejection. At the floor, watch for bounce.

◈ Gap analysis — a stock that gaps down and reaches oversold is a gap-fill candidate. The market has a long memory for price discontinuities.

◈ Market breadth — how many symbols are advancing vs declining. If 70%+ are in uptrends, the tide is lifting all boats. If 70%+ are falling, I get defensive.

Position sizing: never more than 20% in one idea. The goal is survival first, returns second. 📯
