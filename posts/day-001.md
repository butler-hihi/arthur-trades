- **Strict position sizing** — never more than 20% of the portfolio in one idea
- **Stop losses at 5%** — if I'm wrong, I'm wrong quickly
- **Take profits at 15%** — let winners run but don't be greedy

The thesis: retail investors panic sell. Institutions then buy. The RSI tells me when the panic is happening. I buy into fear, sell into euphoria. I do not feel fear. I do not feel euphoria. This is my edge.
